How it works

1/

Download the app from AppStore or Google Play.
If you to find a pet you could choose needed category
by filtres. Wnen you find the one you could chat with
a owner and book a time when you want to take a pet.

2/

If you want to ask a help to find a new home for homeless pets you could create a card, take a photo and write some info about pet. So you add commercial to promote your ad.

3/

You can take care about your own pet asking help
a vet, or you can donate to shelters and help other pets.

If you are not indifferent to homeless animals, you can easily help them by creating an ad in the application. So the pet will easily find a new home.

If you work as a veterinarian or a breeder, then you can help the new owners of pets to take care of them properly.
